About Snuggle & Stroll

I started Snuggle & Stroll because I have a dog I absolutely adore—and I couldn’t imagine leaving her in a kennel. The noise, the stress, the unfamiliar environment… it just never felt right.

So I built the service I wished existed: in‑home, heart‑led pet care from people who treat your animals like their own. Today, Snuggle & Stroll works with a vetted network of 500+ skilled dog owners and pet sitters who understand routines, body language, and the little details that make your pet feel safe.

Whether it’s a quick walk, a midday check‑in, or an overnight stay, our goal is simple: your pet stays relaxed, loved, and at home—while you get to actually enjoy being away.

  • Fully insured sitters in your neighborhood

  • Real‑time updates with photos and notes
